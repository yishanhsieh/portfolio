IU Women Basketball Analysis

Project Overview

  • Role: Data analysis & Chart designer
  • Tool: Excel, Tableau
  • Date: April, 2024
  • URL: Tableau Public
  • Data Source: 2006~2024 NCAA statistics, Her Hoop Stats, Sports Reference, IU Athletics

Introduction

Indiana Women’s Basketball team has been her way toward glory in the past 20 years. This data story aims to reveal the changes of IU’s rank in Big Ten conference, winning percentage, team performance by coach, player’s individual performance, and attendance per game.
